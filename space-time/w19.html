Space-Time Reading Group

The Space-Time Reading Group meets weekly to discuss current research in spatiotemporal statistics and related fields.

In Winter 2019, we are discussing current applied work including research projects led by our very own reading group members.

This quarter's weekly meeting time is still to be determined. Light refreshment will be provided.

This year, Space-Time Reading Group is jointly organized by John Best and Peter Gao. To receive updates about meetings and other upcoming events, please sign up for the listhost.
