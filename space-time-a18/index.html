Space-Time Reading Group

The Space-Time Reading Group meets weekly to discuss current research in spatiotemporal statistics and related fields.

In Autumn 2018, we are reading and discussing Advanced Spatial Modeling with Stochastic Partial Differential Equations Using R and INLA by Elias T. Krainski, Virgilio Gómez-Rubio, Haakon Bakka, Amanda Lenzi, Daniela Castro-Camilo, Daniel Simpson, Finn Lindgren and Håvard Rue (website and Gitbook).

Meetings are on Tuesdays from 12:30 - 1:30pm in the CSSS conference room (Padelford C14A). Light refreshment is provided.

This year, Space-Time Reading Group is jointly organized by John Best and Peter Gao. To receive updates about meetings and other upcoming events, please sign up for the listhost.
